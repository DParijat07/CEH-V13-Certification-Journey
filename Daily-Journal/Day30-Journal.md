# CEH Study Progress

## Module Studied

### Module 09 – Social Engineering

### Topics Covered

* Introduction to Social Engineering
* Human-Based Attacks
* Computer-Based Attacks
* Psychological Manipulation Techniques
* Phishing Attacks
* Impersonation Attacks
* Social Engineering Countermeasures

### Key Learning

Social engineering attacks exploit human psychology rather than technical weaknesses. Attackers often rely on trust, urgency, fear, curiosity, or authority to manipulate victims into revealing sensitive information or performing actions that compromise security.

Strong security awareness programs, verification procedures, and user education remain the most effective defenses against social engineering attacks.
