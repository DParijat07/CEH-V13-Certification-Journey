## Key Learning

### Creeper

* Learned that **Creeper** was the first experimental computer worm.
* It demonstrated self-replication over ARPANET and displayed a harmless message rather than causing damage.
* It showcased the concept of moving between systems using resource-sharing capabilities.

### Reaper

* Learned that **Ray Tomlinson** created **Reaper**.
* Understood that Reaper was designed specifically to locate and remove copies of Creeper.
* Learned that Reaper is regarded as the **first antivirus software** ever created.
* Studied the concept of a **nematode**, a type of malware designed to remove other malware.
* Learned that Bob Thomas' primary project focused on **resource sharing (RSEXEC)**.
* Researched that **API** stands for **Application Programming Interface**.

### Wabbit (Rabbit)

* Learned that Wabbit was among the **first truly malicious programs**.
* Understood that it continuously replicated itself until system resources were exhausted.
* Learned how Wabbit resembles a modern **Fork Bomb (Denial of Service attack)**.
* Studied why Rabbit is not classified as a worm because it does not spread over a network.
* Learned that the name **"Wabbit"** originated from **Elmer Fudd's pronunciation of "Rabbit"** in the Looney Tunes cartoons.
