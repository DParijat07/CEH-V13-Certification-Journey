* Botnets and Attack Infrastructure
* DoS/DDoS Detection

### Key Learning

Denial-of-Service attacks target the **availability** component of the CIA Triad by overwhelming systems, applications, or networks with malicious traffic. Distributed attacks leverage multiple compromised devices (botnets) to generate significantly larger attack volumes, making them more difficult to detect and mitigate.
