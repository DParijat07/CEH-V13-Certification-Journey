## Activities Completed

### 1. CEH Module 04 - Enumeration (Introduction)

Studied the introductory concepts of Enumeration and understood its role in the ethical hacking lifecycle.

#### Key Learnings

* Enumeration follows the scanning phase.
* Enumeration actively extracts information from discovered services.
* Common targets include:

  * SMB
  * FTP
  * SSH
  * SMTP
  * DNS
  * RDP
  * VNC
* Enumeration helps discover:

  * Usernames
  * Shares
  * Hostnames
  * Domain information
  * Network resources
* Enumeration acts as the bridge between scanning and exploitation.
