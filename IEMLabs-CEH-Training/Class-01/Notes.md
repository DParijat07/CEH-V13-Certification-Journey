# What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting systems, networks, applications, and digital data from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage.

---

# Types of Hackers

## White Hat Hackers
Ethical hackers who legally test and improve security systems.

## Black Hat Hackers
Malicious attackers who exploit systems illegally.

## Gray Hat Hackers
Hackers operating between ethical and unethical boundaries.

## Script Kiddies
Beginners who use existing tools without deep technical understanding.

## Hacktivists
Hackers motivated by political or social causes.

## Cyber Terrorists
Attackers who use cyber attacks to create fear or disruption.

## State-Sponsored Hackers
Government-backed attackers involved in cyber warfare or espionage.

# Linux Concepts

## Kernel
The kernel is the core part of the operating system that manages hardware and system resources.

## Shell
The shell acts as an interface between the user and the kernel.

Examples:
- Bash
- Zsh
- PowerShell

---

# Red Team vs Blue Team

## Red Team
Simulates attacks to identify security weaknesses.

## Blue Team
Defends systems and monitors security incidents.

## Purple Team
Combines knowledge from both red and blue teams.
