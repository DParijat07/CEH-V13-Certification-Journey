## Module Overview

This module covers Denial of Service (DoS) and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, their methodologies, attack vectors, tools, detection techniques, and defensive countermeasures.

The objective is to understand how attackers disrupt the availability of systems and how security professionals can detect, mitigate, and prevent such attacks.

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, I will be able to:

- Understand the CIA Triad with emphasis on Availability
- Differentiate DoS and DDoS attacks
- Identify common DoS attack techniques
- Understand botnets and amplification attacks
- Learn DoS detection methodologies
- Explore DoS mitigation strategies
- Perform basic network traffic analysis
- Recommend security controls against DoS attacks
